<p>WHEREAS, Sharon J. Stone is an esteemed public servant who has 
 more than four decades of success with military readiness and health care; and</p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, from 1989 to 1999, Sharon "Sherry" Stone played a 
 pivotal role in implementing centralized medical business operations across 
 what was then United States Army Europe, helping to align the command's 
 personnel, workload, and financial systems; and</p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, as a program manager between 1999 and 2004, Sherry 
 Stone provided crucial medical cost accounting education and training to staff 
 at Army medical treatment facilities, greatly 
 facilitating the Medical Expense and Performance Reporting System; and </p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, Sherry Stone was promoted to senior financial analyst 
 in 2004 and later spearheaded the development of the performance planning 
 program of the United States Army Medical Command (MEDCOM), turning strategy 
 into action and supporting the budget allocation of $6.3 billion for health 
 care delivery; and</p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, in December 2018, Sherry Stone answered the call to 
 lead, develop, and implement the Defense Health Agency's Quadruple Aim 
 Performance Planning process, resulting in the creation of a single, 
 integrated, transparent process to align military treatment facilities with the 
 Defense Health Agency's priorities; and </p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, over the past two decades, Sherry Stone has trained 
 more than 30,000 personnel across the Military Health System, sharing her 
 valuable expertise in order to enhance the Military Health System's performance 
 planning; and</p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, Sherry Stone was recognized in February 2018 by the 
 Chief of Staff of the United States Army for her support of MEDCOM and the 
 United States Army, and she is also a recipient of the Department of Defense's 
 Spirit of Service Award for displaying honor, integrity, and excellence as a 
 public servant within the Department of Defense; and </p>
 
 
<p>WHEREAS, Sherry Stone's professional achievements have had a 
 profound and positive impact on countless lives and are worthy of the esteem 
 and admiration of all Virginians; now, therefore, be it </p>
 
 
<p>RESOLVED by the House of Delegates, That Sharon J. Stone 
 hereby be commended for her exemplary contributions to the Department of 
 Defense and the Military Health System; and, be it </p>
 
 
<p>RESOLVED FURTHER, That the Clerk of the House of Delegates 
 prepare a copy of this resolution for presentation to Sharon J. Stone as an 
 expression of the House of Delegates' high regard for her support of the 
 nation's military and its veterans.</p>
